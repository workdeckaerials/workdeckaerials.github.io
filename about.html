About Working Deck Aerials

The name of the company stems from my career as a seagoing technician on the "working deck" of a research ship. That journey (which I'm still on) has led me to some wonderful places, and given me some really exciting flight opportunities. I'm based out of Spokane Washington (although of course travel is never out of the question), and am availible for a variety of drone flight services. With my own equitment, and flight experience, I look forward to bringing my flight experience to your next project! Please reach out with any questions, and look at some my my example pictures here, or more of a selection on my Flickr and Youtube pages -Croy
